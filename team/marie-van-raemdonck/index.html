Marie Van Raemdonck

Partner

Benefiting from over ten years’ experience in European affairs, Marie leads the consumer goods, energy and environment, and trade practices at Nove, also providing advice to technology clients.

Marie joined Nove in 2013, having previously worked as a consultant at APCO Worldwide’s Brussels office. She specialises in developing engagement and communications strategies to support clients as they deal with European decision-makers and stakeholders to achieve business objectives.

Her wide-ranging experience includes work in communications at the British Council and at the European Commission’s Directorate-General for Agriculture and Rural Development. She also supported the European Peacebuilding Liaison Office (EPLO) as a Policy Officer Assistant.
